Natsuneko Laboratory (hereinafter referred to as "the Operator") uses cookies to collect certain information for the purposes of analyzing customers' website usage and providing customized services and advertisements to individual customers.

**Note: This is an English translation of the original Japanese Cookie Policy. In the event of any discrepancy between the English and Japanese versions, the Japanese version shall prevail.**

## 1. About Cookies

Cookies are a function that stores customers' website browsing information on their computers (devices capable of connecting to the Internet such as PCs, smartphones, and tablets).

Cookies include those set by the Operator (first-party cookies) and those set by third parties in partnership with the Operator (third-party cookies).

## 2. Purpose of Cookie Use

- The Operator uses cookies to improve customer convenience, such as eliminating the need to repeatedly enter the same information when customers browse the website.
- The Operator may use information collected through cookies to analyze customers' website usage (access status, traffic, routing, etc.) and use it to improve the performance of the website itself and to enhance and improve services provided by the Operator to customers.
  - In this analysis, the following tools are mainly used, and information may be provided to the tool providers:
    - Google Analytics (access analysis)
    - Sentry (error tracking)
- In addition, cookies may be used for the delivery of behavioral targeting advertising by advertising delivery service providers with whom we partner.

## 3. How to Reject Cookies

- How to reject all cookies
  - Customers can disable cookies by changing their browser settings. However, if cookies are disabled, some services may not be available.
  - Please contact your browser manufacturer for information on how to change cookie settings.
- How to reject specific cookies
  - Please contact each company for information on how to stop providing information to advertising delivery service providers. You can also check the URLs of the main advertising delivery service providers listed below.
